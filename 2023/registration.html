Webelos Weekend Registration

Join Us for Webelos Weekend!

Get a glimps of all the fun you can have when you join Boy Scouts!

Earn requirements for your Arrow of Light!

Learn leadership and skills that you will use thoughout your life!

Come for the day or camp overnight for our Webelos Weekend event where BSA Troops 280 (boys) and 3280 (girls) give Webelos participants a taste of what fun Scouting can be. We will have many exciting History themed activities.

Webelos and their parent(s) will enjoy a delicious dinner, an entertaining campfire program on Saturday, and a Scout-led Interfaith service Sunday morning.
