Webelos Weekend Registration

Join Us for Webelos Weekend!

Get a glimpse of all the fun you can have when you join Scouts!

Earn requirements for your Arrow of Light!

Learn leadership and skills that you will use thoughout your life!

Webelos Weekend is an overnight camping event where active BSA Troops 280 (Boys) and 3280 (Girls) give Webelos participants a taste of what fun Scouting can be. We will have many exciting Minecraft themed activities. Webelos and their parent(s) will enjoy a delicious dinner, an entertaining campfire program on Saturday, and a Scout-led Inter-faith service Sunday morning.


When:

 November 9th & 10th, 2019

Where:

 Heard Scout Pueblo
 1901 E. Dobbins, Phoenix, AZ 85042

This event is free to all 2nd year Webelos and one (1) parent. Additional attendees are $10 each, to be collected at the event. Saturday night dinner and dutch oven desserts will be provided by the troop. Have lunch before you arrive, and bring any snacks for Saturday. You will need a tent, sleeping bag and warm clothes for this exciting overnight adventure. If you are unable to stay for the overnight, please plan on staying for the campfire program which should end at approximately 8:30 pm.
